Delete My Millionaire – Football GOAT Account

Updated: 25 June 2025

If you want to delete your in-game account and all associated data (scores, progress, cloud saves, analytics identifiers), please send an email from the address linked to your game account to: millionairefootballgoat@gmail.com

Be sure to include “Account Deletion” in the subject line.

What Happens Next

  1. We verify the request by confirming the sender address and, if needed, asking for your in-game user ID (shown in Settings → Profile).
  2. We delete all personal data stored in Firebase Auth, Firestore, and Crashlytics within 7 days.
  3. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Data That Is Kept

Aggregated, non-identifiable analytics (e.g., number of active users, crash counts) may be retained for app-performance statistics.

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at: millionairefootballgoat@gmail.com
